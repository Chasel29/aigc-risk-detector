# AIGC Copyrightability Assessment Platform

Human-AI Co-Creation Research System
面向生成式AI时代的人机协同创作版权认定研究平台

---

## Project Overview

This project is an experimental AI governance and copyrightability analysis platform built around the emerging legal challenges of AIGC (AI Generated Content).

The platform focuses on:

- Human-AI collaborative creation
- Copyrightability assessment of AIGC works
- Prompt creativity analysis
- AI dependency evaluation
- Simulated judicial tendency analysis
- International copyright trends

Rather than simply detecting AI-generated content, the system attempts to construct a research-oriented framework for analyzing whether AI-assisted works may possess copyrightability under different legal perspectives.

---

## Research Background

With the rapid development of generative AI technologies such as ChatGPT, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Claude, and Gemini, traditional copyright frameworks are facing unprecedented challenges.

Core questions include:

- Can AI-generated works obtain copyright protection?
- What level of human participation is legally significant?
- Does Prompt engineering possess originality?
- How should courts evaluate Human-AI collaborative creation?

This project explores these issues through a visualized and interactive research platform.

---

## Core Analysis Framework

The platform is built upon a Four-Dimensional Copyrightability Framework:

### 1. Human Participation

Evaluates:

- Multi-round modification
- Human restructuring
- Creative intervention
- Manual refinement
- Narrative design

### 2. Originality

Evaluates:

- Creative expression
- World-building
- Narrative structure
- Visual aesthetics
- Artistic language

### 3. AI Dependency

Evaluates:

- One-click generation
- Fully automated generation
- Lack of human modification
- Direct AI output usage

### 4. Expression Complexity

Evaluates:

- Structural complexity
- Character relationships
- Multi-layer narrative systems
- Spatial and visual design

# AIGC知识产权研究与辅助分析平台

Human–AI Co-Creation Research & Governance System  
面向生成式人工智能时代的人机协同创作版权认知研究平台

---

## 项目简介

本项目围绕“AIGC生成内容可版权性认知差异及影响机制”展开研究，聚焦生成式人工智能快速发展背景下知识产权治理面临的新问题。

平台以“AIGC知识产权研究与辅助分析”为核心定位，结合案例研究、认知分析、风险识别与可视化展示，探索人工智能时代“技术认知—版权认知—治理态度”之间的关联关系。

本平台并非传统意义上的“AI检测工具”，而是面向AIGC知识产权治理研究的实验性辅助分析平台，重点用于：

- AIGC创作行为研究
- 人机协同创作分析
- AIGC版权认知研究
- 版权风险场景展示
- 平台治理与协同治理研究
- AIGC治理可视化展示

---

## 研究背景

随着ChatGPT、Midjourney、Claude、Gemini、Sora等生成式人工智能技术快速发展，AIGC（AI Generated Content）正深刻改变数字内容生产方式。

人工智能由“辅助工具”逐渐演变为“内容生成主体”，在提升内容生产效率的同时，也对传统知识产权制度带来了新的挑战。

当前围绕AIGC生成内容的：

- 可版权性认定
- 权利归属
- 独创性判断
- 平台治理责任
- 训练数据合法性

等问题，学界与产业界尚未形成统一认知。

本项目尝试通过“实证调查 + 案例研究 + 可视化展示”的方式，对不同主体在AIGC知识产权问题中的认知差异与治理倾向进行研究。

---

## 核心研究框架

平台基于“四维AIGC创作行为分析框架”展开研究：

### 1. 人类参与程度（Human Participation）

重点分析：

- 人工修改行为
- 多轮Prompt调整
- 创意重构行为
- 内容后期加工
- 人类主导创作程度

### 2. 独创性表达（Originality Expression）

重点分析：

- 创作表达特征
- 内容结构设计
- 叙事与语言表达
- 艺术风格呈现
- 创意贡献程度

### 3. AI依赖程度（AI Dependency）

重点分析：

- 自动生成程度
- 一键式生成特征
- AI直接输出占比
- 人工干预程度

### 4. 表达复杂度（Expression Complexity）

重点分析：

- 内容结构复杂性
- 多层表达特征
- 视觉与叙事复杂度
- 创作系统性

# AIGC Copyrightability Research & Governance Platform

Human–AI Co-Creation Research System  
An experimental research platform for copyrightability analysis, AI governance, and Human-AI collaborative creation in the generative AI era.

---

## Project Overview

This project focuses on the research topic:

> "Cognitive Differences and Influencing Mechanisms in the Copyrightability of AIGC-Generated Content"

The platform explores emerging challenges in copyright governance brought by generative artificial intelligence technologies such as ChatGPT, Midjourney, Claude, Gemini, and Sora.

Rather than functioning as a traditional AI detector, the system is designed as an experimental research-oriented platform integrating:

- AIGC copyrightability research
- Human-AI collaborative creation analysis
- Copyright risk scenario studies
- Governance mechanism exploration
- Visualized research presentation
- Experimental cognitive analysis

The project attempts to construct a research pathway combining:

> Technology Cognition → Copyright Cognition → Governance Attitude

within the context of AI governance and digital intellectual property studies.

---

## Research Background

With the rapid advancement of generative AI technologies, AIGC (AI Generated Content) is reshaping digital content production.

Artificial intelligence is gradually evolving from a supportive creative tool into a partially autonomous content-generation participant, bringing significant challenges to traditional copyright systems.

Current debates mainly focus on:

- Copyrightability of AI-generated works
- Human contribution and originality
- Ownership determination
- Platform governance responsibilities
- Legality of training data
- Human-AI collaborative creation standards

This project explores these issues through empirical research, case studies, and visualized governance-oriented analysis.

---

## Core Research Framework

The platform is based on a Four-Dimensional AIGC Copyrightability Analysis Framework.

### 1. Human Participation

Key dimensions include:

- Multi-round Prompt modification
- Human restructuring behavior
- Creative intervention

- Human-led content organization

### 2. Originality Expression

Key dimensions include:

- Creative expression patterns

### 3. AI Dependency

Key dimensions include:

- Degree of automated generation

- AI output dependency
- Human intervention intensity

### 4. Expression Complexity

Key dimensions include:

- Structural complexity

- Visual and narrative complexity
- Systematic creative design
